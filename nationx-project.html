NATIONX

Unified Digital Government Services Platform for Bangladesh

DBMS Project — Full Stack e-Governance Portal

1 / 11

🟢 THE PROBLEM & SOLUTION

Bangladesh has 170+ million citizens interacting with dozens of siloed government agencies — requiring visits to 10+ separate offices for basic documents like NID, Passport, and Land Records.

Before NATIONX

  • Fragmented — 10+ offices for basic docs
  • Paper-based — Records lost or forged
  • No tracking after submission
  • Manual approvals — weeks of waiting
  • Zero citizen interaction platform

After NATIONX

  • Single portal for 20+ services
  • MySQL DB with audit trails & triggers
  • Real-time status tracking
  • Admin dashboard with structured workflows
  • Community groups, complaints, market

Complex Queries

  • WITH ROLLUP — hierarchical location reports with auto subtotals
  • Window Functions — running totals, RANK(), LAG(), 7-day moving avg
